Our Mission

Fair Play, Real Payouts.

Al-Bawaba was founded with a clear purpose: to organize fair, competitive eFootball tournaments that genuinely reward the players who compete in them. Unlike many tournament platforms that take the lion's share of entry fees, we operate on a transparent model where 80% of all entry fees go directly back to the players as prize money.


We exist to bridge the gap between the digital world of online competitive gaming and the physical reality of community life here in Meknes. Our tournaments bring together players from local cafes and online platforms, creating a space where skill speaks louder than anything else. Every bracket is structured to ensure fairness, every result is transparent, and every winner is paid in full.

We bridge the gap between online gaming and real-life community in Meknes. 80% of entry fees return to the players. The remaining 20% covers organization and platform costs — nothing more.

Tournament Rules

How It Works.

Every bracket follows the same rules. No exceptions, no confusion — just clean competition.

Minimum 8 Players

Each bracket requires at least 8 registered players to proceed. This ensures meaningful competition and a proper knockout structure from the very first round.

Maximum 32 Players

Brackets are capped at 32 participants to maintain tournament quality, keep matches running on schedule, and ensure every player gets a fair shot at the prize pool.

20% Organizer Fee

A flat 20% of total entry fees is retained to cover platform, organization, and operational costs. The remaining 80% is distributed entirely to the top-placing players.

Authentic Teams Only

All players must use authentic, official teams in their matches. No custom or edited squads — pure skill with the real teams as they were designed to be played.
